About Compro

IT Services and IT Consulting

Compro Technologies helps EdTech innovators create practical and affordable learning technology solutions. For two decades, startups to large multinational educational publishing organizations have appreciated our ability to help them imagine, develop, and deploy award-winning digital products for millions of students and educators.

It could be a product for significantly cutting the time-cycle for building courseware without involving an army of software engineers. Or, it could be the architecting of a new next-gen publishing platform for a leading publisher with multiple delivery channels worldwide. We are working on some of the key challenges in digital publishing today.

Engagements ranging from publishers like Cambridge University Press and National Geographic Learning to Pearson and Cengage have helped us gain a broad understanding and a depth of insight into the digital publishing space. Our experience ranges across a span of 15+ years in educational technology and publishing.
